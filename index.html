What We Offer

Our Story

A Barbershop Built on Community and Craft

Blake's Barbershop has been a fixture of downtown Hyannis since 2007. Located at 507 Main Street, the shop has built a loyal following across Cape Cod by consistently delivering skilled cuts in a professional and welcoming environment.

The team at Blake's is known for more than just technical ability. The shop prides itself on a friendly atmosphere where every customer, regardless of age or background, feels respected and at ease. From two-year-olds getting their first fades to wedding parties preparing for big days, Blake's handles every visit with care.

Blake's gives back to the local community as well, with the team regularly visiting the Latham Center to provide haircuts to those in need. It is a shop that genuinely cares about the people it serves.
